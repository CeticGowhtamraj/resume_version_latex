Before You Upload

Follow these guidelines to maximize your ATS score

Structure

  • Clear section headings (Experience, Skills, Education)
  • Keep to 1–2 pages max
  • Standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Inter)
  • Avoid tables, text boxes & columns

Contact Info

  • Full name at the very top
  • Professional email address
  • LinkedIn & GitHub URLs
  • City, State / Country

Skills & Keywords

  • Dedicated Skills section
  • Exact tool names (React, Python, AWS)
  • Mirror keywords from job descriptions
  • Don't list buzzwords without context

Experience

  • Dates: "Jan 2022 – Present"
  • Strong action verbs (Led, Built, Reduced)
  • Quantify results ("improved speed by 40%")
  • Avoid vague phrases like "responsible for"
